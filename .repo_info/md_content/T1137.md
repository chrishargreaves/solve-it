# T1137

**ID:** T1137

**Name:** Browser history examination

**Description:**

The examination of artifacts of webpages visited

**Synonyms:**

web history examination, internet history examination, 

**Details:**

These are typically what are displayed in the address bar, with data typically stored within a locally stored database.
