# T1069

**ID:** T1069

**Name:** Browser examination

**Description:**

Recovery of information left from web browsing activity (derived from Oh et al (2011))

**Synonyms:**



**Details:**

This may involve: history, cached items, bookmarks, cookies, saved passwords, form data.

**Potential weaknesses and potential mitigations:**

- [W1113](W1113.md): Misinterpretation a URL located on disk/memory as a web visit _(INAC-AS, MISINT)_
    - M1190: Ensure that the context of recovered URLs is considered during event reconstruction 
- [W1250](W1250.md): Incomplete browser activity extraction due to not considering the browser cache artifacts _(INCOMP)_
    - M1187: Ensure browser cache data is also considered  ([T1138](T1138.md))
- [W1251](W1251.md): Incomplete browser activity extraction due to failure to consider multiple profiles _(INCOMP)_
    - M1188: Ensure all profiles of the web browser are considered ([T1144](T1144.md))
