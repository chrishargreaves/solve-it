**Name:** Browser examination

**Description:**

Recovery of information left from web browsing activity (derived from Oh et al (2011))

**Synonyms:**



**Details:**

**Potential weaknesses and mitigations:**

- W1113: Misinterpretation a URL located on disk/memory as a web visit _(INAC-AS, MISINT)_
    - M1190: Ensure that the context of recovered URLs is considered during event reconstruction 
- W1250: Incomplete browser activity extraction due to not considering the browser cache artifacts _(INCOMP)_
    - M1187: Ensure browser cache data is also considered  ([T1138](T1138.md))
- W1251: Incomplete browser activity extraction due to failure to consider multiple profiles _(INCOMP)_
    - M1188: Ensure all profiles of the web browser are considered ([T1144](T1144.md))
