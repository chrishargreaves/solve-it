# T1069

**ID:** T1069

**Name:** Browser examination

**Objective(s):** Extract artifacts stored by applications

**Description:**

Recovery of information left from web browsing activity (derived from Oh et al (2011))

**Synonyms:**



**Details:**

This may involve: history, cached items, bookmarks, cookies, saved passwords, form data.
